✅ Environment variables (.env) are BETTER than hardcoded URLs
✅ localhost is FASTER and MORE STABLE for development

✅ Docker containers need 30-60 seconds to initialize
✅ ngrok free URLs CHANGE every restart (need to update .env)
✅ System Status page (http://localhost:5173/system-status) is your friend
